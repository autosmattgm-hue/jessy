✦ Quick Answers

Before You Book

A few helpful details so your first appointment feels simple from the first message.

How do I get the fastest quote?

Send your service, inspo photo, preferred date, location area and budget on WhatsApp. For nails, include length and shape. For hair, include length and fullness.

Can I ask for help choosing a style?

Yes. Use Jessy AI for quick recommendations, then send the final idea to WhatsApp so Jessy can confirm the exact service and price.

What should I do before lashes or nails?

Arrive with clean eyes for lashes and avoid heavy oil around the eye area. For nails, arrive with clean hands and send design references before your slot.

Where can I see full pricing?

The dedicated pricing page has nail, hair and lash service ranges. Final prices depend on length, detail, volume and add-ons.
